Development Roadmap

What we're working on to improve Paddle Clubs
  • In Progress Address-level club locations
    Moving from city-level coordinates to actual club addresses so each dot represents a real location you can navigate to.
  • In Progress Individual club profiles
    Each organization will have its own marker and detail panel instead of being grouped by area.
  • Next Club detail pages
    Dedicated pages for each club with photos, schedules, contact info, and member reviews.
  • Next Improved search & filters
    Filter by discipline, skill level, adaptive programs, and distance from your location.
  • Planned Club owner dashboard
    Allow club owners to claim and update their listings with current info, events, and photos.
  • Planned Events calendar integration
    See upcoming races, clinics, and social paddles at clubs near you.
